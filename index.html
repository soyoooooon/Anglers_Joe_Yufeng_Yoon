TRAA TROUT HATCHERY

Rainbow Trout Eggs have Arrived!

This year's allotment of rainbow trout eggs were delivered to the trout hatchery April 16th.
This means we now have both rainbows and browns in the hatchery together!
It's a great time to visit as you can see the trout both in the egg and the fry stage. The TRAA trout hatchery is a popular activity of the TRAA as it's a great teaching tool with regard to the life cycle of a trout.
Those involved with the hatchery get to see firsthand the development stages of the egg and the fry before their final release destinations to area streams.Click Here to see a video of the rainbow trout eggs being brought to the hatchery as well as a close look at the brown trout fry! You can Contact Us or attend a TRAA General Meeting for more info.
